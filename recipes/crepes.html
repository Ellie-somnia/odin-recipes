Crêpes inratables

Credit: M Draa

Description

An impossible to miss recipe to make French crêpes like a pro.

Ingredients

For 15 to 20 crêpes:

  • 250g flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 450ml room temperature milk
  • 2 tbsp rhum or orange blossom water (optional)
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 50g melted butter

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in the microwave and make sure your milk is at room temperature to avoid the creation of lumps later on (you can put it in the microwave very briefly if it's just out of the fridge).
  2. Sift the flour and mix it with sugar and salt. You can replace 50g of flour with 50g of cornstarch for a lighter texture.
  3. Add the eggs, the melted butter and slowly incorporate the milk while whipping vigorously to avoid lumps.
  4. Add the vanilla extract and the rhum or orange blossom water if you wish, then let the preparation rest for 30 minutes at room temperature.
  5. Melt a knob of butter in your pan (not too much) pour about a ladleful of batter in the pan and wait for it to make bubbles or for the edges to unstick from the pan before turning it and cooking the other side.
  6. Reserve your crêpes on a plate on the side while you're cooking the whole batch, and you're done! Bon appétit.
